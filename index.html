Roofing Cost Guides

Real, data-backed cost estimates for every type of roofing project — so you know what to expect before getting a quote.

🏠
Full Roof Replacement
$8,000 – $25,000 avg.
Complete tear-off and installation of new roofing materials. Cost varies by size, material, and pitch. Asphalt is the most affordable; metal and tile cost more but last longer.
Asphalt shingle$8,000 – $14,000
Metal roofing$14,000 – $25,000
Tile / clay$18,000 – $35,000
🔧
Roof Repair
$300 – $3,500 avg.
Minor repairs like fixing a few shingles or patching a leak are relatively inexpensive. Larger repairs involving decking or structural issues cost more.
Minor patch / shingles$300 – $900
Flashing repair$500 – $1,500
Leak & structural repair$1,500 – $3,500
🔄
Re-Roof & Overlay
$5,000 – $12,000 avg.
Installing a new layer of shingles over the existing roof — lower labor cost but not always recommended. Only viable if the existing deck is in good condition.
Small home (under 1,500 sq ft)$5,000 – $7,500
Medium home (1,500–2,500 sq ft)$7,000 – $10,000
Large home (2,500+ sq ft)$9,000 – $12,000
🔍
Roof Inspection
$150 – $400 avg.
A professional inspection gives you a full health report — ideal before buying a home, after a storm, or as a preventive measure every few years.
Standard visual inspection$150 – $250
Drone / thermal inspection$250 – $400
Insurance-grade report$300 – $500
💡 What affects your cost?
Roof size
Measured in "squares" (100 sq ft). Larger roofs cost more.
Pitch / slope
Steeper roofs require more safety equipment and labor.
Material quality
30-year shingles cost more than 20-year but save long-term.
Location
Labor rates vary significantly by state and city.
Season
Spring and fall are peak seasons — prices may be higher.
Contractor tier
Certified, insured contractors charge more — and are worth it.

Our Story

Built to protect every home.

Rooferly was founded on a simple belief: homeowners deserve to find a trustworthy roofer without being pressured, scammed, or overwhelmed.

Why we built Rooferly

The roofing industry has a trust problem. Fly-by-night contractors, aggressive sales tactics, and opaque pricing have made homeowners rightfully skeptical. After a major storm hits, vulnerable families are targeted by unethical operators — and without a reliable way to verify credentials or read real reviews, many get taken advantage of.

We built Rooferly to flip that dynamic. Every contractor on our platform is background-checked, license-verified, and reviewed by real homeowners. Our matching system surfaces the right professional for your specific roof — not whoever paid the most for an ad.
